## Definition
A *real-valued sequence* is a real-valued [[Function]] whose domain is a set of the form $\{n\in\mathbb{Z}: n \geq m\}$. For $n \in \mathcal{D}_a$, $a(n)$ will be denoted by $a_n$ and is called an *element* of the sequence $a$.
