## Content
It's a type of [[Feature Map Fusion|feature fusion technique]] where features, $I_i$ , are concatenated to one another through a $k$ dimension:
$$\begin{equation}
\text{Concat}(I_0, I_1, ... , I_n, \text{dim}=k)
\end{equation}$$
