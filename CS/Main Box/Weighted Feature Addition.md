## Content
It's a type of [[Weighted Feature Fusion|weighted feature fusion technique]], where the feature maps, $I_i$ are added in a weighted fashion:
$$\begin{equation}
\sum_i w_i \cdot I_i
\end{equation}$$
where $w_i$ is the weight associated to the feature map $I_i$
