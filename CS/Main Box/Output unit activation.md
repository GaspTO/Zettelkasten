## Content
It results from applying a linear transformation described by the weights of a layer of [[Artificial Neural Network|neural network]] to the input of that layer:

$$\begin{equation}
	a_j = \sum_{i=1}^{D} w_{ji} + w_{j0}
\end{equation}$$
